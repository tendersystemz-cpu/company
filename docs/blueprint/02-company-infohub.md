# 02 - Company InfoHub Module

Purpose: Company profile and Company Passport module.

Sub-modules:
- Company Master Register
- SSM Info
- People Info
- Office Info
- Finance Info
- Staff Info
- Project Experience
- Evidence Mapping
- Completion Score

Workflow:
Create Company -> Company Master -> Profile Sections -> Evidence Mapping -> Company Passport -> Completion Score -> Readiness Engine.

Tables:
- companies
- company_profiles
- company_contacts
- bank_accounts
- project_experience
- staff_personnel
- field_values

Outputs:
- Company Passport
- Company Profile PDF
- Missing Profile Checklist
